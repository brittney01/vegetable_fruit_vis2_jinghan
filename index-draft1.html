intro

Food has the potential to nurture and support human health, however for many it serves the opposite. It is a massive challenge to feed a rapidly growing population, where 800 million people lack sufficient food and many more do not have access to a high quality diet. With rising challenges such as these, it is vital to understand diet patterns across different regions and the health issues that correlate to them.

recommending diet

In 2019, the EAT-Lancet Commission on Healthy Eating published a report describing the scientifically supported ingredients for a healthy 2500 calorie diet for adults. Their target diets are divided into plant proteins, animal proteins, carbohydrates, and fats and sugars. Although the ideal diet varies from person to person, their goal was to quantify a generally healthy diet that is environmentally sustainable.
